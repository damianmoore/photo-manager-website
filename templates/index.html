Photonix

Photo Manager

Photonix is a photo management application that solves a lot of problems. Once set up it will ingest all the photos in your collection and start building up an image database of everything you could want to search and filter by. It makes your entire collection available to you, whichever device you're using.

It's aim is to be invisible to your existing process of downloading photos from your camera, editing and saving, but to be there whenever and wherever you need to find a photo again. Smart filtering is made possible by object recognition, location awareness, color analysis and this list of these algorithms continues to grow.

Images are everywhere in our lives today but all too often these gems end up as just files and folders on a hard drive — never to be seen again. Professional photographers spend a lot of time on photo editing so it's important that those photos (and associated raw files) can be easily retrieved. Compared to the amount of time spent on a shoot, the time spent in a photo editor such as photoshop or lightroom can be as long, if not more. Photo editing software has evolved a lot over the years but often your photo management tool (if you have one) can feel stuck in the past. If you are struggling to find software that will help manage your photos then we think we may have the solution. Photo organization can be a tough nut to crack. Maybe you let icloud photo library, google photos picasa take charge of your daily snaps but find managing your professional SLR image files a much less slick affair.

What are the benefits to a photo organizer? Well, you can think as your photos as going through a pipeline — first you are going to be out and about with your camera actually taking the photos. After shooting you'll typically download the files onto your computer and do your editing. It's after this editing stage where the waters can get a bit murky and there is no obvious path to take. You'll have some more files now and probably some more folders but where should they live for the long term?

Photonix is photo management software done right. It will organize your photos, raw files and other pictures by importing your existing collection from folders on your computer. It can handle many file formats including raw images from all the main camera manufacturers.
